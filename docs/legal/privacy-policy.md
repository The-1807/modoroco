# Privacy policy — self-hosted foundation

Modoroco currently provides self-hosted software and no hosted service operated by The 1807 is
represented by this repository. Self-hosting operators determine what tenant, session, credential,
and event data they collect and are responsible for notices, lawful basis, security, retention,
and data-subject requests. Modoroco does not sell data. This text requires professional legal
review before any commercial hosted launch.
