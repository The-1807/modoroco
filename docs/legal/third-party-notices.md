# Third-party notices

Runtime dependencies are locked in `uv.lock`; their names, versions, and upstream license metadata
are the authoritative input for release-time notice generation. Notable direct dependencies include
FastAPI, Pydantic, SQLAlchemy, asyncpg, Alembic, Uvicorn, Prometheus Client, structlog, and PySide6.
This summary does not replace their distributed license texts. PySide6 carries LGPL/GPL licensing
conditions that desktop distributors must review and satisfy.
